Brief

Visual identity design for Santalab, a citizen innovation lab, promoted by the Government of Santa Fe. A space that articulates new forms of citizen organisation.

Project Overview

Visual identity

The brand is created from a modular design that incorporates a fun visual play with the letter A. This element transmits the concepts of experimentation, transformation and spontaneous innovation, which are inherent to citizen labs.
